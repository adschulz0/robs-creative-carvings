The story
Twenty Years in the Making

Rob & The PNW

Rob grew up in the Pacific Northwest and has spent his whole life here. The bears, eagles, and salmon that define the region have long influenced his work.

Built Sustainably

We use electric tools throughout the carving process and source wood locally, including reclaimed material that would otherwise go to waste. Every piece is finished with several coats of quality varnish, ensuring they hold up over time and stand up to the elements.

Wholesale Ready

We work directly with gift shops, lodges, and retail buyers across the PNW. We offer reliable inventory, straightforward pricing, and carvings with character behind them.
