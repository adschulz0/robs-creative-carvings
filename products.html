Signature piece

Log Bears

Sculptures of bears nestled into logs carved and finished by hand. Textured fur, expressive faces, and marble eyes that give each one a little life. A natural fit for PNW gift shops, lodges, and outdoor retailers.

Each bear is carved from reclaimed telephone pole end cuts and sealed for durability. They come in a range of sizes and three finishes: natural, black, and cinnamon. No two are exactly alike.

Available sizes: Stump-top (12–20") · Mid log (2–3 ft) · Full log (4+ ft, custom)
